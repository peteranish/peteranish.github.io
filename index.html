Project Highlights

Selected achievements demonstrating our expertise and capability

2000MW Solar Project - Kingdom of Saudi Arabia

Performed comprehensive civil structural engineering reviews as part of the Owner's Engineering team for this massive solar installation. The project included two substations with 115kV double circuit overhead transmission lines and gantries. Each substation featured a 33kV switchgear building, 115kV GIS building, relay and control building, power transformers, cable tunnels, roads, and complete infrastructure.

WTG Foundation Design Evaluations - International

Completed multiple wind turbine generator foundation design evaluations for projects based in India, Vietnam, and other international locations for a major certification body. These projects involved comprehensive structural and geotechnical analysis ensuring compliance with international standards.

Technical Due Diligence - Wind Farms India

Conducted detailed technical due diligence evaluations of two operational wind farms in India for a major consultancy. The scope included comprehensive review of civil infrastructure, foundation design, structural integrity, and risk assessment with mitigation recommendations.
